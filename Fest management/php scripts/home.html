About Codefest

We present you, the biggest coding fest in India Codefest, with its impulsion the year 2011, is the international student fest of PDPM IIITDM, Jabalpur. Spread across a span of five days, the fest provides students from junior and under graduate colleges a chance to participate and explore. Participating and showcasing talent in a coding extravaganza or showing their competitive spirit through coding, Codefest has it all. With immense and growing national and international participation, Codefest aims at being one of the premier fests of the country and an unforgettable experience for all.
